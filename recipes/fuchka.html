FUCHKA

Ingredients

For puri

  • ½ cup Semolina aka suji
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose Flour aka Maida
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon or 1 small pinch of Baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons Water
  • Oil to deep fry the puris

For potato filling

  • 2 large or 3 medium Potatoes, boiled
  • 1 small Onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2-3 green Chillies, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup Coriander leaves, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup boiled yellow Peas (Matar)
  • ¼ cup-soaked Kala chana (Black chickpeas)
  • ¼ cup Tamarind water
  • 1 teaspoon black Salt
  • ½ tablespoon Bhaja masala
  • ½ tablespoon Chaat masala
  • 1 teaspoon Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

Directions

For puri

  1. Take a mixing bowl and add all the dry ingredients and mix.
  2. Add water in small portions to the mix till it forms an elastic dough
  3. Cover dough with wet cloth and let it rest for half hour.
  4. After that, knead the dough for 2 minutes before dividing into two portions.
  5. Take one portion and roll it into thin strips
  6. Use a circular cookie cutter and cut the dough
  7. Warm some oil in medium heat in a pan and once the oil is hot enough, slowly put the dough cutouts into the oil and fry it.
  8. Once the dough puffs up, transfer it to a parchment paper to remove the excess oil
